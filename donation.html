Donation
Thank you for wanting to donate to HelpBuntu. HelpBuntu is designed to give free computer help and advice to anyone who wants it. HelpBuntu upholds the Ubuntu philosophy and so makes no financial gain from its services. There is the option for anyone who so wishes, to make a donation. These donations will go towards the the running costs of the HelpBuntu website. Donations are not compulsory but any amount is greatly appreciated and helps secure the long-lasting future of HelpBuntu. Your donation would not qualify as a charitable or tax deductible donation. All donations are secured via our partner PayPal. If more than required for 1 year is donated then the excess will be carried over and go towards the next year. If you wish at any time to take back your donation then simply contact HelpBuntu with the relevant transaction details, and the transaction can be fully or partially refunded (Please allow time for transactions to clear). Thank you.
